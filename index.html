Why do we need cryptocurrency?

Every tokens or coins existed on crypto space are considered speculative due to the nature of crypto market which is volatile. Unless, they offer long-term visionary project which could bring other benefits for a long term other than speculatively trading it on the market. SMT is not designed to offer a hit-and-run profitability where people commonly buy token at early price on presale and intend only to sell at the price they targeted without any commitment for staying with the project for long term.
